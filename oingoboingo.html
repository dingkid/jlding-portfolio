OINGO BOINGO

"Oingo Boingo" Is a 1lb Plastic Ant (PlAnt) combat bot that was named minutes before its battle. This was a collaborative effort with Ian Bhatt, Albert Lin, and Peter Molzer, members of the Northeastern Combat Robotics team.
The design process began on a collaborative OnShape file. Here, I assisted with the weapon motor shell part of the robot.
